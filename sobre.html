Bio

Lucas Matheus (or Luk) is a small (nerd) freelancer 2D Gamedev born in 1998 in Belém-PA on Brazil, he wanted to make games since 12 and when after he started the Analysis and Development course in Pará State University he created the doujin soft website NightLabs Info, your main specially is Programming and Character Design.

Your hobbies is to make videos and another multimedia contents in "Estudio do Luk" which is another project created since his Highschool (2013-14), your main influences is Nintendo games in general, geek culture, the brazilian culture and recently with japanese creators influences.
